## Section 1 - Non-Technical Description  
The program works as a helper that lets other programs claim temporary control over a named item for a set amount of time while also allowing them to store or retrieve small bits of information attached to that item. When the allotted time expires the claim is released automatically and any stored information is removed unless it has been saved to disk for later use. It waits for messages from other programs over the network carries out actions such as taking hold releasing hold reading writing or erasing data associated with each name keeps track of active claims in memory writes larger pieces of data to files on disk logs its activity periodically cleans up expired entries and reports statistics about its operation every hour.
