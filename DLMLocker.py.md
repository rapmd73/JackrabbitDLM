## Section 1 - Non-Tactical Description  
This tool helps multiple computers safely share access to important files across networks by letting one computer temporarily claim exclusive control over any file; other computers attempting access during this period will wait until control automatically releases after set time limits or gets manually freed early once work completes ensuring no conflicts occur between simultaneous users
