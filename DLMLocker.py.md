## Section 1 - Non-Technical Description  

This program helps multiple users safely share access to important documents across different computers by ensuring only one person can modify each document at any given time; when someone begins working on a document others are temporarily prevented from changing it until they finish their edits or step away after waiting too long-similarly preventing accidental overwrites while allowing others immediate access once available-all while keeping track of who currently holds control through invisible background coordination between machines.
